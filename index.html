Welcome Kings Fans

The LA Kings were founded in 1967 as part of the NHL's six-team expansion. They have a storied history with multiple Stanley Cup victories and a passionate fan base.

The Kings have won the Stanley Cup twice, in 2012 and 2014. They have also produced several Hall of Fame players and have consistently been competitive in the Western Conference.

Notable players include Wayne Gretzky, Luc Robitaille, and Jonathan Quick. Current stars include Anze Kopitar and Drew Doughty, who have been instrumental in the team's recent successes.
